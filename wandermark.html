Welcome to Wandermark

Maps live in a Google Drive folder you choose, so they're saved, available on any device, and easy to share with friends.

After signing in, you'll pick (or create) a Drive folder — your maps are saved there automatically. Got a link to someone else's map? Open it directly and you can view it without signing in.
